Electroencephalography (EEG)

We are our brain.

My work evaluates how brain activities vary across time and space when humans use their cognitive abilities.

My toolbox includes approaches of EEG analysis, such as event-related potentials (ERPs) and event-related spectral pertubations (ERSPs), but also more complex connectivity analysis such as event-related phase-amplitude coupling (ERPAC) and machine learning and decoding techniques like multivariate pattern analysis (MVPA) and the generalization across time (GAT) method.
Over the last years, I have recorded many human EEG recordings during virtual reality games. See a few examples on my YouTube channel.
