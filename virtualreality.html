Virtual Reality (VR)


Spaceship environment

In collaboration with Psious/Amelia (Spain) and the University of Wurzburg (Germany), we developed the Spaceship environment.
It is currently used by the research unit INSERM 1114 (Strasbourg, France) to help diagnose and treat patients with schizophrenia.


Play with Robots!

In collaboration with the research unit INSERM 1114 (Strasbourg, France), I have developed a virtual environment to evaluate the temporal skills of video game players.


I speak therefore I do

In collaboration with the University of Plymouth (UK), I have developed a virtual environment to assess whether labeling novel objects (in VR) affects brain processes of object visualization and manipulation.
Spoilter alert: it does!
Check out the research article "Labels strengthen motor learning of new tools"


Object knowledge shapes brain visual processes

In collaboration with the University of Plymouth (UK), I have developed a virtual environment to evaluate how our knowledge about objects influences brain processes.
Check out the research article "Tool use and function knowledge shape visual object processing"
